I authorize model-based processing of my personal data for activities such as:

- This consent is limited to model providers, local services, and APIs that I personally run, configure, or directly authorize.

- Sensitive data should only be sent to hosted providers when I intentionally enable that integration.

- This consent may be narrowed, replaced, or revoked by me at any time.
